Hierarchy

Animals are scientifcally categorized into a seven-rank hierarchy:
  1. Domaine
  2. Kingdom
  3. Phylum
  4. Class
  5. Order
  6. Family
  7. Genus
  8. Species

Vertebrates V.S. Invertebrates

There are two different different living styles of animals. They move differently, get their energy differently, and so much more. Here are the differences:
Vertebrates
    Vertebrates are animals that have a backbone. Here are some examples:
  • Dogs
  • Birds
  • Fish
Invertebrates
    Invertebrates are animals that don't have a backbone. Here are some examples:
  • Insects
  • Crabs
  • Worms
